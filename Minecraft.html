How you start out

When you first install minecraft you probbaly will be tempted to start a world. When you start a world you have 3 options on the java versian. You have Survival, Creative and Hardcore. You will create your world in your desired gamemode. If you choose survival you will spawn into your world with nothing in your inventory. You will start of by punching a tree and collect wood. Once you cut down a tree you will craft some basic tools. When night comes you need to hide. At night mobs/enimies will spawn and try and attack you. World options and Gamemodes

In minecraft there are 4 gamemodes. Survival, Creative, adventure and spectator.

Survival mode is one of the allowed starting modes. Survival mode allows you to explore with freedom. You can change your game diffculty and can use cheats if desired.

Hardcore is one of the allowed gamemodes but is more challenging. Hardcore is locked at the ahrdest diffculty and cant use cheats. This is mostly used in 100 day hardcore videos.

Creative mode has more freedom and can build anything without problems

Spectator and adventure mode are only able to be used in a world with cheats enabled.

heres how to enable them with cheats /gamemode survival (player name) /gamemode creative (Player name) /gamemode spectator (player name) /gamemode adventure (player name)
